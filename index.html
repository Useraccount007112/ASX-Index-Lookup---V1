How it works

01

Search any company

Enter a company name or ASX ticker code. The app recognises both — "BHP" or "BHP Group" will both work.

02

Live web search

The Anthropic AI uses real-time web search to find the most current index membership data from ASX and S&P sources.

03

Index classification

Results show all indices the company belongs to — the indices are nested, so ASX 100 members are also in ASX 200, 300, and All Ords.
